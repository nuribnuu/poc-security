PoC: Audio Permission Origin Spoofing

This PoC tests if the browser correctly attributes the origin (domain) when requesting microphone access from an about:blank popup.

Testing Steps:
1. Click the button above to open a blank popup window.
2. Inside that popup, click the "Trigger Audio Permission" link.
3. Observe the system permission dialog

Vulnerable: The dialog says "Allow [Browser Name] to record audio?" WITHOUT showing your domain.
Secure: The dialog clearly mentions your domain (e.g., your-site.com).
